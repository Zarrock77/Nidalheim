## 1. Project Presentation

### Context

Nidalheim is a semi-open world action-adventure game set in a Nordic universe. The player explores a hostile world composed of boreal forests, snowy mountains, and traditional wooden villages, while surviving the elements and creatures that inhabit these lands.

### Objectives

The main objective of Nidalheim is to deliver an immersive experience where quests are dynamically generated by an artificial intelligence based on context: player position, nearby NPCs, progression, and world state. This approach aims to provide high replayability and emergent narrative unique to each playthrough.

### How It Works

The game is built on three pillars:

1. **Exploration**: The player evolves in a semi-open world divided into zones, with a Nordic village as the central hub.

2. **Combat**: A combat system based on Unreal Engine's Gameplay Ability System (GAS), featuring weapon-based and character-based abilities.

3. **Dynamic AI**: A cloud-hosted artificial intelligence generates contextual quests and NPC dialogues, creating a unique narrative experience.

### Platform and Tools

- **Engine**: Unreal Engine 5
- **Target Platform**: PC
- **Version Control**: Perforce Helix Core
- **AI**: Cloud service (quest and dialogue generation)

## 2. User Role Table

| ID | Role | Description |
|----|------|-------------|
| U1 | **Player** | Main user who explores the world, fights enemies, interacts with NPCs, and completes quests. Manages survival (hunger, thirst, health). |
| U2 | **NPC (Non-Player Character)** | Neutral or allied characters present in the village. Provide dynamically generated quests and interact through AI-generated voice dialogues. |
| U3 | **Enemy** | Hostile creatures populating the world. Possess combat AI and patrol or attack the player on sight. |

## 5. Out of Beta Scope

The following elements are **not** part of the beta scope and will be implemented after Greenlight:

- Additional biomes (mountains, fjords, extended forests)
- Dynamic weather system (storms, fog)
- Full day/night cycle
- Additional quest types (escort, delivery, investigation)
- Crafting system
- Save/load functionality
- Multiple enemy types
- Ally and companion system
- Final optimization and polish
